    welcome

    Here is a local music mananager that gives you the ability to pull the vocals and instrumentals of your songs, along with other features.
    When you downloaded this application a folder was created to handle songs.
    This folder is songs-mp3 in the audio-input folder.
    Add songs to this folder to play and utilize this app.

    Here you can split songs into instrumentals.

    "If you would like to extract the vocals of a song, select the song from the list.
    The vocals will be provided below to download so then it will update and appear in the songs list.
    From there you can add it to a playlist or any other reason"
